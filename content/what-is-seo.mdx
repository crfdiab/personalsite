## What Is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the process of optimizing your website to improve its visibility and ranking on search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo. The goal of SEO is to make your website more attractive to search engines, so it can appear higher in the search results when people search for relevant keywords or phrases.

SEO is important because it helps your website reach more people and can lead to more traffic, leads, and sales. It can also help you stand out from your competitors and establish your brand as a leader in your industry.

There are many different aspects to SEO, including keyword research, on-page optimization, off-page optimization, and technical SEO. By understanding these concepts and how they work together, you can improve your website's visibility and ranking on search engines.
